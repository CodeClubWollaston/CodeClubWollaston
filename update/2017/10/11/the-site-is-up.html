The site is up!

Oct 11, 2017 • Sam Drage

Welcome to Wollaston School Code Club’s new blog for the PiWars 2018 competition!

It may be a little bare at the moment, but we hope to fill it with glorious content very soon, as well as frequently update it as we build our robot: The W.A.S.P. The “Wollaston’s Annoying Sound Producer” is the follow-up to our previous entry “Whoop-Whoop!”, who glided smoothly into 16th place. We have learned from last year, and we aim to do better.

We hope to do more than just text content, with videos and images from the team being a possibility, so stay tuned.
